Before You Begin
General Rules

Applicable to every participant across all events. Read thoroughly before submitting.

01Competition conducted virtually through video / photo submission 📩
02Participants must be currently enrolled students of the institution
03Time limit for all performance events: strictly 2–3 minutes
04Videos must be clear, properly lit and properly visible
05Excessive editing, filters, or special effects are not allowed
06Participants must ensure good audio and video quality
07Content must be original — not copied from others
08No inappropriate language, vulgarity, or offensive content 🚫
09All submissions must be sent before the deadline
10Judges' decision will be final and binding
